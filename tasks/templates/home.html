Task Manager App

Welcome to our Task Manager app! This application is designed to help you stay organized and manage your tasks efficiently.

User Registration:

Create your personalized account to unlock the full potential of our app. By registering, you gain access to a secure space where you can save, organize, and track your tasks.

Task Management:

  • Create Tasks: Easily add new tasks with titles, descriptions, and due dates. Specify the importance level to prioritize your to-do list.
  • Modify Tasks: Edit your tasks at any time. Update titles, descriptions, due dates, or change the importance level to reflect changes in priority.
  • Delete Tasks: Remove tasks from your list when they are completed or are no longer relevant.

Task Viewing Options:

  • View Completed Tasks: Track your accomplishments by reviewing the tasks you've successfully completed. Celebrate your progress and achievements.
  • Filter by Importance: Quickly identify and focus on high-priority tasks by using the importance level filter. This helps you allocate your time and energy efficiently.

Task Details:

  • Title and Description: Each task allows you to provide a concise title and a detailed description, ensuring clarity on the task's purpose and requirements.

User-Friendly Interface:

Our app features an intuitive and user-friendly interface, making task management a seamless experience. Enjoy a clean and organized dashboard that helps you navigate effortlessly.

Stay Organized, Stay Productive:

With our Task Manager app, you can take control of your daily activities, enhance your productivity, and keep track of your accomplishments. Whether you're a professional juggling work tasks or a student managing assignments, this app is designed to simplify your life.
